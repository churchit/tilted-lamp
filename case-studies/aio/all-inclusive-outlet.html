Project Description

All Inclusive Outlet is a discount all-inclusive travel package provider. They asked Tilted Lamp to help them better merchandise their top travel offerings online, focusing on the Caribbean, Mexico, Costa Rica, and Panama. The engagement included detailed data analytics and competitive analysis. Once we arrived at an agreed upon customer hypothesis, balanced with the business need to merchandise specific resorts and destinations as Hot Deals, I delivered updated key page designs, improved UI, simplified user journeys, and a iterative, interactive prototype for both web and mobile.
